About Us

The Smith Fund was founded on January 8th, 2024 and is based in South Carolina. The Smith Fund is managed by Jacob Smith and Gavin Smith, two passionate wealth managers with a shared vision of delivering superior investment returns for clients. Jacob and Gavin are committed to providing personalized, transparent, and thoughtful management of capital. Though we are young and the fund was born only a year ago, we have learned timeless lessons in that short span of time and that learning will not stop. As ambitious and determined young men, we are not stopping until our clients are satisfied and more. Our first year goal was to grow the fund AUM to $10,000, which we exceeded by 65% by growing the fund to $16,500 in our first year. We have no plans on stopping this momentum. Much of our philosophy is built on the greats such as Benjamin Graham, Peter Lynch, and Warren Buffet. Below we go more in depth with our philosophy and how we pick our stocks.

Our Statistics + 2024 (1st Year) Performance
